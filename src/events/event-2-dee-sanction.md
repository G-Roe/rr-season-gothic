Edinburgh. December 1598. The King’s seasonal revels are disturbed, The King is not himself. Conspiracy and treachery are in the air. The King’s trusted Chancellor, Patrick, the Master of Gray calls our party from their feasting and dancing. 

Players are agents of the Crown, riding out into the swirling snow to resolve the King’s situation. The days are short. The nights are long. And the veil between this world and the other is thin as midwinter approaches.
