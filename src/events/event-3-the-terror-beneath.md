In The Terror Beneath, you play members of The Gold Tiberius Society, a group dedicated to investigating intrusions of the Otherworld into Britain of the 1920s. Inspired by the works of Arthur Machen and powered by the GUMSHOE system, this is Scott's most recent game published by Osprey Books.

Machen's tales blend folklore and the occult; exploring themes of hidden realities, forbidden knowledge, and the intrusion of ancient, supernatural forces into the mundane world. Lovecraft later incorporated Machen’s themes into his own cosmic mythos.
